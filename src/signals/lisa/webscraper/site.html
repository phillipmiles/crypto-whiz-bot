    our RANGE has been $45,000 to $65,000. Ranging is a postive sign of an asset stabilizing and gaining momentum, and essentially these areas become support and resistance. Today we look at a simplistic form of trading called OB.

    OB – Order Block. It’s a simple Supply Zone (Resistance) and Demand Zone (Support), Trading strategy, used by many traders – especially those on twitter.

    Breakouts and Breakdowns

    Traders can enter in the direction of a breakout or breakdown from a trading range. To confirm the move is valid, traders should use other indicators, such as volume and price action.
